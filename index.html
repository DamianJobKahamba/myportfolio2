BIOGRAPHY

Epidemiologist • Laboratory Scientist • Proficient in SAS / R / Python • Proud Husband and Father

I'm Damian Job Kahamba, a public health scientist and MPH candidate at Florida A&M University. I focus on epidemiology and biostatistics through data-driven program improvement and combining statistical analysis with practical systems that help teams act quickly and fairly.

Before graduate school, I served at Tanzania's National Public Health Laboratory, progressing from Health Laboratory Scientist to Head of the HIV Molecular Testing Section. I led ISO-15189–aligned quality systems, coordinated proficiency testing and sample referral networks for HIV viral load and early infant diagnosis, and supported supervision for quality assurance. I also contributed to operational readiness through training, including SARS-CoV-2 genome sequencing & bioinformatics (Africa CDC/ILRI).

At FAMU, I worked as a Graduate Assistant supporting community environmental restoration requests (Zendesk), presenting at environmental events, and right now, I am contributing to technical work in the Soil Science Laboratory. This complements my epidemiology training by strengthening cross-disciplinary problem-solving and stakeholder communication. Methodologically, I'm comfortable with SAS, R, Smartsheet, and Python. I have experience in executing lightweight data programs that have helped to bring actionable insights.

My mission is simple: improve public health through evidence-based methods and the implementation of software methods to simplify and improve public health work. I'm active in professional networks (APHA, FPHA, and campus leadership) and collaborate readily across labs, agencies, and community partners to translate evidence into policy and practice.

Outside work, I'm a bilingual communicator (Swahili/English) who values mentorship, systems thinking, and service. I'm open to roles in epidemiologic analysis, program evaluation, public-sector analytics, and health-tech teams where rigorous methods and real-world impact meet. Background details on earlier training and leadership are available in my CV.
